Blue Box Simulator

App Description

Have a Time Machine Inside Your Phone!

You may know that police telephone blue box that is bigger on the inside and can travel anywhere in time and space. Now you can have it on your phone!

After starting up, tap on the screen to show the controls (and a nice date and time label).

Tap on Space Throttle for take off and start your journey through the time vortex, and tap again to land.

You can travel to any date and coordinates or play some minigames, and watch your blue box materialize or dematerialize.

And last but not less, this app is completely free and ad-free!

If you want to support us, just leave a review telling us what you would like to see in the next update!
